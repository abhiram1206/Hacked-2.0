All You Need to Know About #Hacked2.0

Welcome to #Hacked2.0, an exhilarating hackathon embedded within the vibrant atmosphere of the 67th Milestone Fest - our annual celebration of innovation and creativity.

About #Hacked2.0

In the heart of our continually evolving industry, #Hacked2.0 is more than a hackathon; it's an immersive experience designed to encourage innovation, networking, and ideation. With a compelling problem statement and an environment buzzing with code and logic, participants will have a unique platform to showcase their skills and creativity during this 24-hour hackathon.

Theme: Unleashing Creativity

#Hacked2.0 embraces an open theme, allowing participants to choose their own problem statements and delve into the realms of innovation. This approach empowers them to explore their creativity, pushing boundaries to find solutions that truly matter.

Participation Criteria

Teams: 2-4 members

Composition: Members can be from the same or different years and colleges

Eligibility: Enrolled in a UG/PG course in any college/university

Rules

Technology Freedom: No restrictions on the technologies used for development.

Originality Matters: Plagiarism of any type will not be entertained.

Final Decisions: Decisions made by the judges and the organizing committee are considered final.
